Hi there! My name is Caden Petersen. I am currently studying Audio and Music Production at Illinois State University. This website is a final project for one of my major classes, but it also doubles as a portfolio site for my work. So far during my time at ISU, I have composed a couple of songs and numerous assignment songs. I also am currently on the executive board of an RSO called Redbird Studios. Redbird Studios is a student led recording studio that gives students of all majors the opportunity to record or produce their own work. Members gain experience in recording, mixing, and producing. In addition to Redbird Studios, I have also been involved in ISU's Big Red Marching Machine, as well as pep band, university band, and also Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia Fraternity.
